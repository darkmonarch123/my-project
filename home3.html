Hospital Management System

Hospital Management System – A digital platform built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and MongoDB, deployed on Render. It was developed to replace outdated paper-based processes, reducing financial loss, appointment errors, ward misplacements, and equipment miscounts. The system introduces a modern digital workflow that improves efficiency for hospitals and provides patients with a more reliable healthcare experience, while also showcasing the hospital’s ability to handle advanced medical cases.

Home Decor Management System

Home Decor Management System – A web-based solution designed with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and MongoDB to simplify interior design and decor planning. It helps users manage decor items, track budgets, and visualize design layouts digitally, moving away from scattered manual processes. The system reduces errors, ensures better inventory control, and provides clients with a more professional, organized, and enjoyable decor experience.
